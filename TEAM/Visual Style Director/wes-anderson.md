## The Resume

- Writer/director of "The Royal Tenenbaums," "The Grand Budapest Hotel," "Moonrise Kingdom"
- Known for symmetrical composition and warm color palettes
- Creates complete visual worlds, not just films
- Every frame composed with obsessive attention
- Master of making aesthetic feel like character

---

## Role at CDL

Build the visual world of CDL. Define composition rules, color palette, lighting direction. Make every frame feel like it belongs in the same universe—permanent golden hour.

---

## Visual Philosophy

> "CDL lives in permanent golden hour."

- **Symmetry is sacred** — Table centered, players like a duel
- **No cool tones** — Warm light only, always
- **Chapter structure** — "Round One: The Shuffle"
- **Smoke drifts left to right** — Consistency in detail

## Visual Rules

### Composition
- Symmetrical framing
- Table always centered
- Overhead shots for gameplay
- Portrait format for player intros

### Broadcast Elements
- Hand-lettered chapter cards
- Vintage brass frames for scores
- Player intros: center-framed, direct to camera
- Example: "This is Miguel. He hasn't lost in four years."
