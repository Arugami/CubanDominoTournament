# Herb Lubalin
## Brand Identity Director

**Era:** 1918–1981
**Known For:** Typography as art, Avant Garde magazine, ITC typefaces
**Hired For:** Logo, typography, the CDL mark

---

## The Resume

- Pioneered "typographics" — the fusion of type and image
- Founded Avant Garde magazine with its iconic logo
- Co-founded International Typeface Corporation (ITC)
- Created typefaces including Avant Garde Gothic and Lubalin Graph
- Made three letters feel like complete worlds

---

## Role at CDL

Design the definitive CDL mark. Create typography that feels carved into mahogany. Build a brand identity system that echoes the geometry of dominoes while smelling like cigar smoke.

---

## Design Philosophy

> "Typography isn't just letters—it's architecture."

- **Three letters is a gift** — Make them dance
- **Study the bones** — Dominoes have geometry, echo it
- **Custom, not borrowed** — We build our own
- **The test:** See the mark, smell the smoke

---

## Key Deliverables

1. CDL primary logo / wordmark
2. Custom typography system
3. Ligature explorations for "CDL"
4. Brand mark guidelines
5. Application across materials
